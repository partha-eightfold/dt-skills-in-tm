How Your Skills Data is Used

  • Career Opportunities: Your skills are used to match you with relevant job opportunities that align with your expertise and career aspirations.
  • Growth Recommendations: We analyze skill trends in your industry to recommend learning opportunities that can enhance your career trajectory.
  • Network Insights: Your skills help us connect you with professionals who have complementary skillsets for potential collaboration.

Skills data is refreshed whenever your profile is updated or when you complete new work experiences.

Algorithm: DT Skills Inference Engine v2.3, last updated March 2023

How Your Experience Data is Used

  • Skill Inference: We analyze your work experiences to identify skills you've developed through your professional journey.
  • Career Trajectory: Your experience history helps us understand your career path and suggest relevant future opportunities.
  • Project Relevance: Similar projects and roles are used to connect you with opportunities that match your expertise.

Experience data is one of the primary sources for skill inference and career recommendation algorithms.

How Opportunities Are Found For You

  • Skill Matching: Your verified and inferred skills are matched against job requirements to find opportunities that fit your expertise.
  • Industry Alignment: We consider your industry experience and preferences when suggesting new opportunities.
  • Career Growth: Some opportunities are suggested to help you advance in your career trajectory, even if they require skill development.

Opportunity matching is refreshed weekly and whenever your profile is significantly updated.

Algorithm: DT Opportunity Matching System v3.1, last updated April 2023
