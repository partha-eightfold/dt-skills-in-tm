Job Overview

As a Senior Data Scientist, you will lead data science initiatives, develop machine learning models, and collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive business insights. You'll be responsible for translating complex data problems into actionable solutions that impact our product and business strategy.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and develop advanced machine learning models to solve complex business problems
  • Lead data science projects from conception to production implementation
  • Collaborate with engineering, product, and business teams to drive data-informed decisions
  • Mentor junior data scientists and contribute to building a strong data culture
  • Present findings and recommendations to executive leadership
